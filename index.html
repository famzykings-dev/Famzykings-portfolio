FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to develop a website?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A standard business website typically takes 4-6 weeks from kickoff to launch, while larger e-commerce or custom web apps can take 8-12 weeks.

What is the pricing for your web development services?

Pricing depends on scope and features. Starter sites begin at $2,500, business sites range from $5,000-$15,000, and complex platforms are quoted custom. We'll provide a detailed proposal after our discovery call.

What types of websites do you create?

We build corporate sites, e-commerce stores, portfolios, landing pages, web apps, blogs, membership sites, and PWAs. If it runs in a browser, we can build it.

What are other services that you offer?

Beyond web development: SEO, performance optimization, hosting setup, API integrations, and ongoing maintenance/support plans.

Do you provide post-launch maintenance?

Yes. We offer monthly care plans covering updates, backups, security monitoring, uptime checks, and content edits. You can also book us hourly for ad-hoc support.

What do you need from me to get started?

To kick off, we need your goals, target audience, content or copy, brand assets like logo and colors, examples of sites you like, and access to domain/hosting if you have them.
